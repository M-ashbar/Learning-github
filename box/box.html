SPECIFICATIONS OF SUPERS CARS

SUPER CAR

BUGGATI CIRON

All Chiron models are motivated by an 8.0-liter 16-cylinder powerplant. This beast of an engine employs four turbochargers to generate a mighty 1500 horsepower and 1180 lb-ft of torque. Bugatti claims that the Chiron makes the dash from zero to 60 mph in a mere 2.3 seconds, and it has a top speed of 261 mph.

 SUPER CAR

LAMBOGHINI SVJ 63

SVJ 63 features the most powerful series of power plant, V12, produced till date by Lamborghini, pumping an increase in power to 770 hp (566 kW) at maximum 8,500 rpm. The SVJ 63 outputs 720 Nm of torque at 6,750 rpm, while a dry weight of just 1,525 kg gives the SVJ 63 a weight-to-power ratio of 1.98 kg/hp.1

 SUPER CAR

FERRARI LAST'S V8

The F8 Tributo uses the same engine from the 488 Pista, a 3.9 L twin-turbocharged V8 engine with a power output of 720 PS (530 kW; 710 hp) at 8000 rpm and 770 N⋅m (568 lb⋅ft) of torque at 3250 rpm, making it the most powerful V8-powered Ferrari produced to date.
